Detection The Spam Emails Using Natural Language Processing And Machine Learning (Feb 2022 - June 2022)

Email Spam has become a major problem nowadays, with the Rapid growth of internet users, Email spam is also increasing. People are using them for illegal and unethical conduct, phishing, and fraud. Sending malicious links through spam emails can harm our system and can also seek into your system. Creating a fake profile and email account is much easy for the spammers, they pretend like genuine people in their spam emails, these scammers target those peoples who are not aware of these frauds. So, it is needed to Identify those spam mails which are fraud, this project will identify those spam by using techniques of machine learning, this paper will discuss the machine learning algorithms and apply all these algorithms to our data sets, and the best algorithm for the email spam detection having best precision and accuracy.

Sentiment Analysis In Marketing: Negative Review Detection In Product Feedback (Sep 2022 - Jan 2023)

This projet is made by a team, me and my collegue. Sentiment analysis (SA) is a process of identifying and categorizing views and opinions expressed in the text by examining huge amounts of data. This paper will summarize the process of Sentiment Analysis and provide the methods and results of the most recent studies. It will introduce the SA project "DeGatto" and provide an argumentative reasoning for the chosen NLP, DL and ML models and methods. The content will describe the progress, timeline and the upcoming goals of the project as well as recently made technical changes.

Exploring Analogical Proportions Between and Within Knowledge Graphs (Sep 2022 - June 2023)

This projet is made by a team, me and my collegue. Semantic annotation is the process of attaching labels or metadata about concepts to a text in order to define the meaning of parts of the text. When this is done it creates a knowledge graph which defines the relationship between these parts. There are manyuses for these knowledge graphs such as chatbots, fraud detection and data governance.There are different ways in which to automatically semantically annotate this text, whichwill be discussed.
